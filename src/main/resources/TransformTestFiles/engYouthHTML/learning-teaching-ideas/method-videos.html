Teaching Videos

Below are videos from actual classrooms that show teachers using methods that are suggested in the learning activities in the Come, Follow Me outlines. These videos demonstrate how you can involve youth in searching the words of the prophets, and help them think about, share, and apply what they are learning. Some of the videos show how to effectively use visuals.
