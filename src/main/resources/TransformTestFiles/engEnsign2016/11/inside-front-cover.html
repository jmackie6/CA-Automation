“‘And he who receiveth all things with thankfulness shall be made glorious; and the things of this earth shall be added unto him, even an hundred fold, yea, more’ [D&C 78:19; emphasis added].

“I have felt that transformation of growing gratitude for blessings and a love of God increasing across the Church. It seems to accelerate among members of the Church in times and places where there are trials of their faith, where they have to plead to God for help to even carry on.”

President Henry B. Eyring, First Counselor in the First Presidency, “Gratitude on the Sabbath Day,” Ensign or Liahona, Nov. 2016, 101.
