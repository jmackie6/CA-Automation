“Essential to the plan is our Savior, Jesus Christ. Without His atoning sacrifice, all would be lost. It is not enough, however, merely to believe in Him and His mission. We need to work and learn, search and pray, repent and improve. We need to know God’s laws and live them. We need to receive His saving ordinances.”

President Thomas S. Monson, “The Perfect Path to Happiness,” Ensign or Liahona, Nov. 2016, 80–81.
