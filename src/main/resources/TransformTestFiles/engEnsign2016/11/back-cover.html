“We are blessed to have the truth. We have a mandate to share the truth,” said President Thomas S. Monson during the 186th Semiannual General Conference of the Church. “Let us live the truth, that we might merit all that the Father has for us. He does nothing save it be for our benefit. He has told us, ‘This is my work and my glory—to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man.’”
