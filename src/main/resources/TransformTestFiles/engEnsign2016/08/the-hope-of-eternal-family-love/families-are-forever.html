Families Are Forever

Because of Jesus Christ’s Atonement and the restoration of priesthood power to seal families, we can live with our families forever! What do you love about your family? Follow these instructions to make this paper chain to celebrate your family.

  1. Fold a piece of paper in half twice so you have one long strip.

  2. Draw a person with hands reaching the folded edges.

  3. Cut the person out. Don’t cut where the hands touch the folded edges.

  4. Unfold it. Write or draw something you love about each family member.

  5. Tape multiple chains together if you have a larger family!
