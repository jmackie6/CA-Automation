“Rest in Peace,” Ensign, August 2016, 55

Rest in Peace

By L. Susan Dobmann

The author lives in Utah, USA

O beloved, now departed

from the world, sweet release.

Cares of life, pain and sorrow

harm no more, rest in peace.

Spirit fled to realms eternal,

with the Savior to repose.

Watching, waiting, till I follow,

reuniting at life’s close.

O departed, loved so tender,

now with loved ones far away.

In the arms of Heavenly Father,

never leave, always stay.

Child of God, loved forever,

may your memory never cease.

O beloved, now departed

from this world, rest in peace.
