“Come, All, unto the Temple,” Ensign, June 2016, 72–73

Come, All, unto the Temple

Words by Toni Thomas

Music by Brett Stewart

With conviction

1. Come, all, unto the temple,

Where holiness abides,

Where hands are consecrated

And hearts are purified.

Through covenants of power,

Our imperfections yield;

The mystery of godliness

In temples is revealed.

2. Come, all, unto the temple,

Where perfect love abounds,

Where hearts are tuned together,

One sweet and solemn sound.

As family forever

Together we are sealed,

The mystery of godliness

In temples is revealed.

3. Come, all, unto the temple,

Where peace unmeasured flows;

From wells of living water

Drink solace to your soul.

As heavy hearts unburden,

As broken hopes are healed,

The mystery of godliness

In temples is revealed.

4. Come, all, unto the temple,

Where endless truth is found

In perfect course, unwav’ring,

Of one eternal round.

As we’re endowed with knowledge,

There from the world concealed,

The mystery of godliness

In temples is revealed.
