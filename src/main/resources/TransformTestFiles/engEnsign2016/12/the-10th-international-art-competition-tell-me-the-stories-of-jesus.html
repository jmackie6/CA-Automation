The 10th International Art Competition: Tell Me the Stories of Jesus

Following is a sampling of art from the 10th International Art Competition that was recently on display at the Church History Museum in Salt Lake City, Utah. Ninety-eight pieces of art were selected from among the 944 entries from 40 countries around the world. Artists could choose any story from the life of our Savior Jesus Christ as the subject for their creation. Interpretations of these stories found their way into paintings, drawings, sculptures, ceramics, photography, collages, needlework, stained glass, and digital art. You can view the exhibit online at lds.org/go/10art.
