“Visions of Christmas,” Ensign, December 2016

Visions of Christmas

Words by Don Staheli

Music by Jim Kasen

With wonder

1. Visions of Christmas fill my mind

And dance before my eyes.

I see a world of joy

For ev’ry girl and boy

And feel the love of God,

And feel the love of God.

2. Visions of Christmas shine so bright.

They speak of long ago,

When Jesus came to earth,

And angels sang His birth,

And Wise Men brought Him gifts,

And Wise Men brought Him gifts.

3. Visions of Christmas in my heart,

I understand them now.

All men will live again,

And Christ will come to reign,

And He will bring us peace,

And He will bring us peace.

4. Visions of Christmas filled with love

For God and for all men.

Gone will be hurt and fear;

I’ll feel my Savior near,

And He will hold me close,

And He will hold me close.
