In Church Magazines

Ensign: We all want to be happy. Learn about seven principles from the Book of Mormon that show us how we can (page 16). See also “Changed by the Book of Mormon” (page 34).

New Era: Elder Robert D. Hales teaches us how we can hear God’s voice through the scriptures (page 2). Also, youth get advice on making good use of time (page 12) and five ways to feel good in five minutes (page 24).
