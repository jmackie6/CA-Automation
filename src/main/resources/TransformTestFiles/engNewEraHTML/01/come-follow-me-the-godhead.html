Come, Follow Me

The Godhead

Understanding our relationship to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost can help us and lift us up in so many ways. Many speakers at the October 2016 general conference helped expand our understanding of the Godhead. For instance:

• “Gratitude on the Sabbath Day,” by President Henry B. Eyring

• “Sharing the Restored Gospel,” by Elder Dallin H. Oaks

• “‘Come, Follow Me’ by Practicing Christian Love and Service,” by Elder Robert D. Hales

• “Serve,” by Elder Carl B. Cook

• “A Witness of God,” by Elder Neil L. Andersen

• “The Master Healer,” by Carole M. Stephens

Find these and more at conference.lds.org.
